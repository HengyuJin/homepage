Welcome!

I am a master's student in Artificial Intelligence at Carnegie Mellon University. My work focuses on AI systems and networked intelligence, especially secure edge networks, LLM-inference-enabled service systems, computer vision, smart healthcare, and IoT applications.

Before CMU, I completed a dual B.Sc. degree program in Computer Science at Southwest Jiaotong University and the University of Leeds. My recent research formulates deployment and protection problems for security-aware network function chains and small-scale inference agents in edge environments.

I enjoy building systems that connect rigorous problem formulation with practical implementation. I am particularly interested in AI systems that are reliable, resource-aware, and useful in real-world environments.
